DubTech Service and Sales Policies

Repairs Policy:

  1. Responsibility for Damages:
    • DubTech is not responsible for any damages that occur after the repair service is completed.
    • Repairs are not provided free of charge unless the damage is a result of our service.
  2. Right to Refuse Service:
    • We reserve the right to refuse service for any reason.
    • If we determine that the damage is purposeful or recurring, service may be refused, and future service eligibility may be affected.

Prebuilt Systems Sales Policy:

  1. All Sales Final: All sales of prebuilt systems are final and non-refundable.

  2. Accidental Damages:
    • We do not cover damages caused by accidents, weather, or any actions by the user. We do cover damaged if the part itself failed due to it being at fault.
    • Parts over $200 will not be covered unless determined that the damage is our fault.
  3. Claiming Free Repair Benefits:
    • To claim any free repair benefits, proof of purchase with the date of purchase must be provided.
    • For prebuilts with bad parts not caused by us, we may cover it once, provided the old part is returned with proof of purchase.
  4. Repairing Damage Caused by DubTech:
    • If damage is caused by our service, we will provide a free repair for the affected part with no limitations.

Expectations and Payment Policy:

  1. Meeting Expectations:
    • We strive to meet your expectations through clear communication before finalizing any purchase.
  2. Payment Methods:
    • All transactions are to be made through Cash App or in-person payment.
    • Down payments are typically 60% of the total cost, with the remainder due upon completion of the service.
  3. Integrity and Honesty:
    • DubTech promises to uphold integrity and honesty in all dealings with customers.
    • We are not responsible for any prebuilt or repair that does not meet your expectations, though we will make every effort to discuss and confirm your requirements before finalizing the purchase.
