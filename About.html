About DubTech

DubTech, an independent small business nestled in Stanton, Kentucky, is the brainchild of Wyatt Dalrymple. Fueled by a lifelong dream of entrepreneurship, Wyatt has embarked on the exciting journey of establishing his small business as of November 2023. While envisioning future growth that spans into the millions, Wyatt recognizes that the current focus lies in step oneâ€”laying the foundation and cultivating a steady flow of clients. Wyatt's dream extends beyond mere business success; it revolves around providing the cheapest and best services in the confines of a small town. In Stanton, where options for computer repairs and prebuilt systems may be limited, Wyatt and DubTech emerge as the local solution. The "Dub" in DubTech is an homage to Wyatt, with the "W" representing his name, while the "Tech" underscores the company's commitment to all things tech-related. At DubTech, we are not just here to fix problems; we're here to redefine local tech services, ensuring accessibility and excellence for everyone in the community.

In addition to his entrepreneurial spirit, Wyatt Dalrymple brings a wealth of expertise to DubTech through his professional certifications. Holding certifications such as IC3 (Internet and Computing Core Certification) and PC PRO demonstrates Wyatt's commitment to maintaining a high standard of knowledge and proficiency in the ever-evolving tech landscape. Currently working towards obtaining CompTIA A+, Wyatt recognizes the importance of staying at the forefront of technology. These certifications not only validate his skills but also showcase his dedication to providing top-notch services to DubTech clients. The pursuit of CompTIA A+ is a testament to Wyatt's commitment to mastering essential IT skills, including hardware, software, and troubleshooting. By investing in ongoing learning and certification, DubTech ensures that its clients receive services backed by the latest industry standards, positioning Wyatt as a reliable and knowledgeable resource in the field.
